| Version | Supported |
|---|---|

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue, **please do not open a public GitHub issue**.

Contact me directly:

- **Telegram:** [@Fireway727](https://t.me/Fireway727)

I will respond as soon as possible and work on a fix. Once resolved, the vulnerability will be disclosed publicly with credit to the reporter (unless you prefer to stay anonymous).
